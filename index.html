Mentorship Programme

About the Mentorship

Our mentorship program pairs early-career researchers with experienced professionals in the field of medical imaging and computer-assisted interventions. Mentors provide guidance, career advice, and research direction to help mentees navigate their academic and professional journeys.

Benefits include:

  • One-on-one guidance from experts in your specific area of interest
  • Networking opportunities with leading researchers
  • Research collaboration possibilities
  • Career development advice

Apply for Mentorship

We welcome applications from PhD students and early-career researchers who are looking for guidance in their research and career development.

Mentorship matches will be made based on research interests and career goals.

Applications are open until [Mentorship Application Deadline].

Challenge

MICCAI Summer School Challenge 2025

Participate in our exciting medical image analysis challenge! Teams will work on solving a real-world problem in medical imaging using state-of-the-art techniques. The challenge provides an opportunity to apply what you learn during the summer school and compete with peers from around the globe.

Challenge details:

  • Problem statement will be announced during the summer school
  • Teams of 3-5 participants
  • Access to datasets and computational resources
  • Mentorship support throughout the challenge
  • Prizes for top-performing teams
